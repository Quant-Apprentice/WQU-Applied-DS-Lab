# WQU-Applied-DS-Lab

## **Quant Journey: From CPA to Data Science**

Welcome to my research repository. I am a Finance major at Hanyang University (South Korea), currently preparing for the **USCPA** and pivoting towards a career in **Quantitative Finance**. This repository documents my 16-week journey in the **Applied Data Science Lab at WorldQuant University (WQU).**

#### Objective
To bridge the gap between **Fundamental Accounting (USCPA)** and **Data-driven Modeling**. My goal is to develop robust financial models for risk management and investment strategies, with a particular focus on the Tokyo financial market.

#### Tech Stack
* **Language**: Python 3.x

* **Libraries**: Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly

* **Core Competencies**: Machine Learning, Time-Series Analysis, Statistical Inference
